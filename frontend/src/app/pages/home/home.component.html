Aims to develop an advanced Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) system designed for medical documentation. Focused on accurately transcribing doctor-patient conversations, the system addresses challenges like accent variations among diverse English speakers and enhances contextual understanding within the medical domain. By building the ASR model, we aim to improve transcription accuracy, ensuring reliable medical records and streamlined clinical workflows.
